WHAT WOMEN'S HORMONE OPTIMIZATION MEANS

Definition: Women’s hormone optimization is a comprehensive medical service that balances sex hormones, thyroid levels, and metabolic markers to resolve symptoms like fatigue, brain fog, and weight gain.

Optimization goes far beyond replacing estrogen. It is a whole-system approach that evaluates how your sex hormones, thyroid, stress response, metabolism, sleep, and body composition interact.

We do not practice "symptom chasing." Instead, we use medical evaluation, personalized strategy, and consistent tracking to address the root causes of how you feel.

Our goal is to help you navigate hormonal shifts with clarity and control, rather than just "getting through it."

PERIMENOPAUSE VS. MENOPAUSE (AND WHY SYMPTOMS CAN START YEARS EARLY)

Perimenopause

The transition years leading up to menopause. Hormones fluctuate wildly, often causing symptoms like sleep disruption, mood swings, and cycle irregularity years before your period stops.

Menopause

Defined medically as 12 consecutive months without a menstrual cycle. At this stage, hormone production has stabilized at a lower baseline.

THE PROBLEM WITH "NORMAL"

It is a common frustration: you feel significant changes in your body, yet your labs come back "normal."

This happens because standard reference ranges are population-based averages, not optimal functional targets. Furthermore, a single lab snapshot can be misleading because women's hormones fluctuate dynamically across cycles and life stages.

We focus on your individual baseline, tracking trends over time, and placing your data in the context of your symptoms.

The Moonshot Difference: Unlike standard care, we use data-driven diagnostics to treat how you feel, not just to check a box on a standard lab range.

COMMON QUESTIONS

What is perimenopause and how do I know if I’m in it?

Perimenopause is the hormonal transition leading to menopause, often starting in a woman's late 30s. Symptoms include cycle changes, sleep disruption, and mood swings, occurring years before periods stop.

What’s the difference between women’s hormone optimization and a med spa?

Moonshot Medical and Performance is a clinic practicing Performance Medicine, not a cosmetic med spa. We focus on internal physiology and metabolic health using comprehensive diagnostics led by licensed medical providers.

Do I need labs if my symptoms are obvious?

Yes. Symptoms reveal how you feel, but labs reveal the root cause. We test thyroid, cortisol, and sex hormones to distinguish between issues like iron deficiency and hormonal imbalance.

Why do doctors say my labs are normal?

Standard "normal" ranges are population averages designed to detect disease, not optimization. We use functional ranges to identify why you feel "off" even when standard tests look fine.

Is hormone optimization only for menopause?

No. We treat women in perimenopause and even earlier. If you experience fatigue, weight gain, or cycle irregularity, we optimize your health regardless of age.

Is this covered by insurance?

We’re out-of-network with most plans. Many patients use HSA or FSA funds, and we can provide documentation for reimbursement where applicable.

How long does it take to notice changes?

Relief from hot flashes can happen quickly. Deep metabolic repair and energy stabilization typically require 3–6 months of consistent, data-driven care.

What if I’m sensitive to treatments?

We prioritize safety by measuring first. Our providers adjust protocols based on your specific lab data and feedback to ensure treatments work with your body.

How do you personalize care?

Every plan is unique. We build your treatment on your specific bloodwork, medical history, and lifestyle goals, avoiding cookie-cutter protocols.
