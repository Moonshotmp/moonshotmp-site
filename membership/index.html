MEMBERSHIP FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Why membership instead of one-time labs?

Optimization works best when you can measure, adjust, and track trends over time — not just take a single snapshot. One-time labs can be useful, but membership creates follow-through, repeat measurement, and a plan that evolves with your data.

What’s included in the membership?

Membership is designed to give you ongoing access to performance-focused care and tracking. What you get depends on your plan, but typically includes guided interpretation of your data, ongoing monitoring, and member pricing on services and diagnostics.

Do I have to start treatment to be a member?

No. Membership can be used for evaluation, clarity, and tracking — even if you never start a protocol. If treatment is ever discussed, it’s only when medically appropriate and always optional.

Who is membership best for?

Membership is a good fit if you care about energy, recovery, and long-term performance; feel “off” even when you’re doing the right things; want real data instead of guesswork; and value tracking trends over time (not just one lab draw).

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

Do you take insurance?

We’re a performance-focused clinic, and most optimization-style care is not covered by insurance unless you meet specific clinical thresholds. That said, we do accept HSA/FSA, and we can provide receipts or documentation upon request. If you’re specifically looking for insurance-based care for a diagnosed medical condition, we’re happy to point you in the right direction.
