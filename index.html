What is performance medicine?

Performance medicine is a proactive, data-driven approach to healthcare that focuses on optimizing how the body functions over time. Rather than waiting for disease to appear, it uses advanced diagnostics, individual baselines, and longitudinal tracking to guide decisions around hormones, metabolism, body composition, recovery, and resilience.

A DIFFERENT MODEL OF CARE

Performance medicine is proactive, not reactive.

Instead of waiting for problems to appear, we measure early, interpret data in context, and make informed decisions aimed at long-term health, energy, and resilience.

This framework applies whether the goal is hormone balance, metabolic health, body composition, or recovery.

Do I need abnormal labs to work with you?

No. Many patients have been told their labs are “normal” but still feel off, inconsistent, or underperforming. We interpret results in context—your symptoms, history, and goals—and compare them to your personal baseline, not only broad population ranges.
