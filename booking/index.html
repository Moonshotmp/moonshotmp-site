Initial Performance Consultation

No guesswork. Just data. We use blood work and diagnostics to build a clear plan for hormones, body composition, recovery, and long-term performance.

Who This Is For

  • 01. Men and women seeking data-driven hormone optimization (TRT/HRT) guided by blood work.
  • 02. Individuals working on body composition and metabolic health (including medical weight loss / GLP-1s).
  • 03. High performers optimizing energy, recovery, and longevity with peptides and evidence-based supplementation.

The First Visit

Duration: 30–45 Minutes

  • Comprehensive medical history and symptom review.
  • Labs and diagnostics ordered (and drawn when appropriate).
  • Body composition analysis.
*No guesswork. Your plan is built after we review objective lab data.

What To Have Ready

  • Valid Photo ID (Driver's License).
  • Complete your intake form before your visit (we’ll send it after you book).

Before You Book

Do I need blood work before my first appointment?
No. Blood work is typically ordered after your initial consultation if appropriate. We’ll review your goals, health history, and determine which labs make sense for you.
What happens during my first visit?
Your first visit includes a comprehensive consult, body composition assessment, and a review of your health goals. If clinically appropriate, lab work may be ordered the same day or shortly after.
How long does the first appointment take?
Plan for approximately 45–60 minutes for your initial visit.
Do you accept insurance?
Moonshot Performance operates as a self-pay performance medicine clinic. This allows us to offer more comprehensive, proactive care without insurance limitations.
Is this clinic only for TRT?
No. While testosterone optimization is a common service, we also work with patients on metabolic health, fat loss, energy, recovery, peptides, GLP-1s, and longevity-focused optimization.
Who is this clinic a good fit for?
Moonshot is best for individuals who value data-driven health optimization, want clear answers, and are proactive about performance, body composition, and long-term health.
Can women book an appointment?
Yes. We work with both men and women on hormone optimization, metabolic health, body composition, and performance goals.
What if I’m already on TRT or working with another clinic?
That’s fine. We regularly work with patients transitioning care or looking for a more thorough, performance-focused approach.
How soon can I get started after my first visit?
Timelines vary based on labs and clinical findings, but many patients move forward within 1–2 weeks after their initial consultation.
Is booking online secure?
Yes. Online booking is handled through our secure medical record system and is fully HIPAA-compliant.
