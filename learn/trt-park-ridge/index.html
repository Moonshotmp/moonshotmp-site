COMMON QUESTIONS

Is TRT the same as steroids?

No. TRT restores testosterone to normal, healthy levels under medical supervision. It's a completely different thing from anabolic steroid abuse, which involves supraphysiologic doses without monitoring. Read our full comparison.

How do I know if I need TRT?

Symptoms like persistent fatigue, low libido, brain fog, muscle loss, and mood changes can indicate low testosterone. But you need blood work to know for sure. We test 60+ markers to get the full picture—not just total testosterone. Review the full symptom list.

Why not just use an online TRT clinic?

Online clinics prescribe based on minimal testing and video calls. They don't track body composition, don't test 60+ biomarkers, and don't see you in person. You deserve more than a prescription—you need a protocol with real oversight and measurable outcomes.

How quickly will I feel results?

Most men notice improved energy and mood within 2-4 weeks. Libido changes typically follow at 3-6 weeks. Body composition changes (muscle gain, fat loss) become measurable on DEXA at 3-6 months. Full optimization often takes 6-12 months of protocol refinement.

Do you accept insurance?

We're a direct-pay clinic, which means no insurance middleman dictating what we can test or treat. This lets us run comprehensive panels and design protocols based on what's optimal for you—not what an insurance company approves. Many patients use HSA/FSA funds.
