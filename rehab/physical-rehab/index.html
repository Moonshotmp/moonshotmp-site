WHO IT'S FOR

Recovering from a sprain, strain, or surgery? We'll rebuild your strength and movement systematically.

Chronic Pain

Dealing with ongoing issues that keep coming back? We'll address the root cause, not just the symptoms.

Performance

Want to move better, lift more, or prevent future injuries? We'll optimize your movement patterns.

THE PROCESS

1

Assess

Comprehensive movement assessment to identify limitations, weaknesses, and pain drivers.

2

Treat

Manual therapy, dry needling, and other techniques to reduce pain and restore mobility.

3

Rebuild

Progressive exercise to build strength, stability, and resilience in the affected areas.

4

Return

Gradual return to activity with sport or activity-specific training as needed.
