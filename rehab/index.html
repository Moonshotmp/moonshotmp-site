COMMON QUESTIONS

What should I expect at my first visit?

We start with a thorough evaluation—your history, movement assessment, and identifying what's causing your pain. Treatment may include spinal adjustments, hands-on therapy, and exercises to do at home. Mild soreness afterward is normal and typically fades within a day.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

No referral needed in Illinois—you can see Dr. Michael directly.

What should I wear or bring?

Wear comfortable, loose clothing that allows you to move freely. Bring any relevant medical records and imaging (X-rays, MRIs).

How many visits will I need?

It depends on your condition. Our goal is to get you better and teach you to stay that way—not keep you coming back indefinitely. Most patients see significant improvement within 6-8 visits.

Do you accept insurance?

We are a cash-pay clinic with transparent pricing. We can provide superbills for potential out-of-network reimbursement.
