Compliance & Guardrails

We operate a transparent, compliant diagnostic access model. This protects your business and ours.

  • No Referral Fees or Kickbacks: You do not receive a commission for sending clients. You profit by bundling the service into your own program at retail value.
  • Flat Wholesale Pricing: Every partner pays the same Fair Market Value (FMV) wholesale rate.
  • Medical Control: Moonshot Medical performs the test and retains sole discretion over medical interpretation and reporting.
  • Scope of Practice: Partners (coaches/trainers) do not order or medically interpret diagnostics. You use the data to inform fitness/nutrition programming only.
  • Volume Agnostic: Wholesale pricing does not fluctuate based on referral volume.
