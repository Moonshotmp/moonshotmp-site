WHAT ARE PEPTIDES?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids—essentially small proteins that signal your body to do specific things.

Your body makes peptides naturally. Therapeutic peptides work by enhancing or mimicking these natural signaling pathways.

Different peptides have different effects: some promote healing, some enhance growth hormone release, some improve sleep or cognition.

Peptides Can Help With:

  • • Injury recovery and healing
  • • Gut health and inflammation
  • • Sleep quality
  • • Growth hormone optimization
  • • Sexual health

COMMON QUESTIONS

Are peptides safe?

When sourced properly and used with medical oversight, therapeutic peptides have good safety profiles. We use pharmaceutical-grade compounds from reputable sources.

How are they administered?

Most peptides are subcutaneous injections (like insulin). Some can be taken orally or as nasal sprays. We'll teach you proper technique.

How long until I see results?

It varies by peptide. Sermorelin effects like improved sleep may be noticed within days to weeks. PT-141 effects are typically noticed within hours of administration.
