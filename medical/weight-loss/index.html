WHY DIETS FAIL

You've tried dieting. Maybe it worked for a while. Then it didn't.

COMMON QUESTIONS

How much weight can I expect to lose?

Clinical trials show average weight loss of 15-20% of body weight over 12-18 months. Individual results vary based on starting point, adherence, and lifestyle factors.

What are the side effects?

Common side effects include nausea, which typically decreases over time with gradual dose increases. We start low and titrate slowly to minimize side effects.

Will I regain the weight if I stop?

Weight regain is possible without lifestyle changes. That's why we focus on nutrition, body composition, and building habits—not just the medication.

Am I a candidate?

GLP-1 medications are appropriate for adults with a BMI ≥30, or ≥27 with weight-related conditions. We evaluate each person individually.
