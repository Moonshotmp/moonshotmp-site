COMMON QUESTIONS

Is hormone therapy safe?

Current research supports the safety and benefits of hormone therapy when started appropriately and monitored properly. We use bioidentical hormones and follow evidence-based protocols.

When should I consider hormone therapy?

If you're experiencing symptoms that affect your quality of life and testing shows a hormonal component, therapy may help. We evaluate each person individually.

What are bioidentical hormones?

Bioidentical hormones are structurally identical to the hormones your body produces naturally. They're derived from plant sources and processed to match human hormones exactly.
