WHAT WE DO DIFFERENTLY

Comprehensive Labs

We test 60+ biomarkers—not just testosterone or thyroid. Hormones, metabolic markers, inflammation, cardiovascular risk, nutrients. The full picture.

Why? Because low testosterone might be caused by thyroid dysfunction, or inflammation, or insulin resistance. You can't optimize what you don't understand.

DEXA Body Composition

The scale tells you almost nothing. DEXA tells you exactly how much fat, muscle, and bone you have—and where.

This matters especially for weight loss. You want to lose fat, not muscle. DEXA lets us track that precisely and adjust your protocol accordingly.

In-Person Care

We're a real clinic in Park Ridge, IL. We draw your blood, run your DEXA, and see you face-to-face.

This isn't about being anti-technology. It's about building real relationships and providing hands-on care that telehealth can't replicate.

Performance Lens

"Normal" lab ranges are based on general populations—including sick people. We interpret results through an optimization lens.

Being "normal" isn't the goal. We want you functioning optimally—energy, body composition, cognition, longevity.

WHEN MOONSHOT MIGHT NOT BE RIGHT FOR YOU

We're not the right fit for everyone. Here's when another option might make more sense:

You just want a quick prescription

If you already know exactly what you want and don't care about comprehensive testing or monitoring, a telehealth clinic will be faster and possibly cheaper upfront. We take a more thorough approach.

You're not local to Chicago/northwest suburbs

We're an in-person clinic. If you're not able to come to Park Ridge for labs, DEXA scans, and consultations, we're not the right geographic fit.

You want the cheapest possible option

When you factor in comprehensive labs and DEXA tracking, our pricing is competitive. But if rock-bottom cost is the priority over thorough care, there are cheaper options.
