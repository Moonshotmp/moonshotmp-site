COMMON QUESTIONS

Does Moonshot take my insurance?

For rehab services (chiropractic, physical rehab, dry needling, shockwave): We accept Blue Cross Blue Shield PPO. For medical services (DEXA, blood panels, hormones, weight loss): These are self-pay, but we can send prescriptions to your pharmacy if your insurance covers medication.

Why doesn't the medical side take insurance?

Insurance-based medicine is reactive—focused on treating disease, not optimization. By operating outside insurance, we can spend more time with you, order comprehensive testing, and focus on helping you feel your best rather than just "not sick." This model also allows us to offer transparent, predictable pricing.

Can I submit receipts for reimbursement?

Some patients with out-of-network benefits can submit receipts to their insurance for potential reimbursement. We provide detailed receipts with appropriate codes. Check with your insurance about out-of-network coverage.

Do you offer payment plans?

Our monthly membership programs are already structured as ongoing payments rather than large upfront costs. For one-time services like DEXA scans or blood panels, payment is due at time of service.
