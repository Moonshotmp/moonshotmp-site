What This Panel Is

This is not a standard check-up. While traditional bloodwork looks for disease, the Moonshot Comprehensive Panel analyzes key biomarkers to evaluate how your body is functioning right now. By looking at multiple systems together—metabolism, hormones, lipids, and inflammation—we move beyond broad population averages to establish your personal baseline for optimal performance.

50+ Actionable Markers

Biomarkers By System

A. Cardiovascular & Lipid Health

Heart health, endurance, and longevity

Includes: Total Cholesterol, LDL, HDL, Triglycerides, ApoB, Lipoprotein(a).

Why It Matters: We go beyond standard cholesterol to measure the actual number of particles in your blood (ApoB) and genetic risk factors (Lp(a)).

Explains: Cardiovascular endurance issues and long-term heart risk.

B. Metabolic Health & Glucose

Energy systems and fuel efficiency

Includes: Fasting Glucose, Hemoglobin A1c, Fasting Insulin.

Why It Matters: This reveals how efficiently your body processes fuel. Fasting insulin often rises years before blood sugar, giving us an early warning sign.

Explains: Energy crashes, stubborn body fat, and difficulty building lean muscle.

C. Male Hormones & Signaling

Drive, vitality, and anabolic potential

Includes: Total & Free Testosterone, SHBG, LH, FSH, Prolactin, Estradiol, DHEA-S.

Why It Matters: We look at the total amount of testosterone available and how well your brain is signaling your body to produce it.

Explains: Libido, motivation, recovery rate, mood, and muscle retention.

D. Thyroid Function & Pace

Metabolism and temperature regulation

Includes: TSH, Free T4, Free T3, Thyroid Antibodies.

Why It Matters: The thyroid controls the "pace" of your metabolism. A complete panel ensures the signal is being sent and received.

Explains: Unexplained weight changes, sensitivity to cold, and sluggishness.

E. Inflammation & Recovery

Systemic stress and immune status

Includes: hs-CRP, Immune/Inflammation patterns (CBC).

Why It Matters: High inflammation suggests the body is fighting stress, injury, or infection, which halts performance progress.

Explains: Joint pain, poor recovery from workouts, and general fatigue.

F. Nutrients & Cellular Support

Foundational fuel

Includes: Vitamin D, Vitamin B12, Electrolytes, Liver & Kidney Function.

Why It Matters: These are the co-factors required for energy production and hormone synthesis.

Explains: Cognitive function, bone health, and daily energy levels.
