What This Panel Is

Analyzing 50+ biomarkers, this panel connects hormones, metabolism, and heart health to pinpoint fatigue and weight shifts across every life stage.

Biomarkers by System

50+ Actionable Markers

A. Cardiovascular & Lipid Health

Includes: Cholesterol, LDL, HDL, Trigs, ApoB, Lp(a), hs-CRP

Beyond basic cholesterol, we measure particle number and genetic risk to explain stamina, heart resilience, and inflammation.

B. Metabolic Health & Blood Sugar

Includes: Fasting Glucose, A1c, Fasting Insulin

Monitoring insulin sensitivity helps prevent stubborn weight gain and energy crashes before blood sugar ever rises.

C. Female Hormones & Life-Stage

Includes: Estradiol, Progesterone, Testosterone, FSH, LH

We map hormone balance against your life stage to explain cycle irregularities, hot flashes, sleep issues, and mood shifts.

D. Thyroid Function & Metabolic Pace

Includes: TSH, Free T4, Free T3, Antibodies

Checking antibody status and conversion efficiency ensures your metabolism is active, explaining hair loss, coldness, and fatigue.

E. Inflammation & Stress Load

Includes: hs-CRP, CBC (Immune Patterns)

Differentiating between healthy training load and chronic stress helps explain fluid retention, joint pain, and slow recovery.

F. Nutrients & Cellular Support

Includes: Vitamin D, Vitamin B12, Electrolytes, Protein

Assessing key co-factors for hormone production helps explain low energy, weak immunity, and poor sleep quality.
